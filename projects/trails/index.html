Trails Overhead

About the project

An interactive installation that explores the concept of presence and perception beyond physical architectures. It consists of a custom made force resistive sensor mat which detects and stores the trails of people's movements on it. These trails are visualized in real time on the floor below, through a ceiling-mounted projection screen. This effectively creates the experience of looking through the ceiling, as if looking at the soles of those occupying the same space directly above.
